FACTS ABOUT KEANU

Some Interesting Facts About Keanu

  1. Keanu has been described as "a workaholic, charming and an extreme introvert".
  2. In 2010 an image of Keanu sitting on a park bench with a sad face went viral. An unofficial holiday named "Cheer up, Keanu Day" on June 15th was declared.
  3. Keanu's Hollywood Star is located at 6801 Hollywood Blvd and near other stars including Donald Trump and Kermit Frog.
